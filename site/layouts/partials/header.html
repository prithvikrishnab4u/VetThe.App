Making informed decisions about SaaS applications requires understanding their identity and access management capabilities. VetThe.App provides a comprehensive comparison of SSO, SCIM, MFA, and compliance features to help security teams, IT admins, and procurement professionals evaluate vendors quickly and accurately.

What we track: Single Sign-On support and protocols, SCIM user provisioning capabilities, multi-factor authentication methods and enforcement options, SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance certifications, and the minimum pricing tier required for each feature.
