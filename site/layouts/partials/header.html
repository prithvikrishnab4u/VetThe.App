VetThe.App

Community-driven comparison of identity security features across SaaS applications.

Project Overview

VetThe.App aggregates technical data regarding Identity and Access Management (IAM) capabilities for various SaaS vendors. It serves as a central reference point for security teams and administrators to verify supported features, such as SSO protocols and SCIM provisioning, without needing to navigate individual vendor documentation.

Data is community-verified and updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

Tracked Capabilities

SSO (SAML/OIDC) SCIM Provisioning SOC 2 & ISO 27001 MFA Enforcement Pricing Tiers
