VetThe.App

Community-driven comparison of identity security features.

We aggregate identity security data.

VetThe.App aggregates technical data regarding Identity security capabilities for various SaaS vendors. It serves as a central reference point for security teams and administrators to verify supported features, such as SSO & MFA protocols support, SCIM provisioning capabilities, and compliance certifications, without needing to navigate individual vendor documentation.

Data is community-verified and updated regularly to ensure accuracy.
