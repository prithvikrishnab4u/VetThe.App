Columns Explained

  • SSO: Single Sign-On support and which tier it's available on.

Tier Meanings

The tier indicates the MINIMUM plan level where the feature is available.

  • Free Available on free tier without payment.
  • Paid Available on mid-tier paid plans (Pro, Business).
  • Enterprise Only on highest tier, usually requires sales contact.
