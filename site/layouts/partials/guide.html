Tier Meanings

The tier indicates the MINIMUM plan level where the feature is available.

  • free: Available on free tier without payment
  • paid: Available on any paid plan (Pro, Business, Team, etc.) but not enterprise-only
  • enterprise: Only available on the highest tier, usually requires contacting sales
